About

Hello, I'm Tiffany! A former cake designer with a passion for creating products people can enjoy. (Cake as in edible delights. Not to be confused with Cake (C# Make) a build-automation utility.) I am actively transitioning to a career in web development.
Like many people my age, I grew up on the internet. I developed an interest for HTML, CSS and JavaScript during this time and took a few classes in high school. After 11 years in the hospitality industry I decided to take a new direction and looked back on coding. I noticed that there is a surprising amount of overlap of skill in both trades. Some of these transferable skills include, attention to detail, time management, collaboration and problem solving, just to name a few. With this in mind, it rekindled my passion for coding.
I started my new journey by completing NuCamp's Full Stack Bookcamp. After graduating, I have taken Udemy courses and created personal projects to help further prepare myself for the profession. As of now, I am actively looking for a coding position to get some real world experience. I am ready to take on the role of a Front End Developer!

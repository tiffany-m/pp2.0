About

Hello, I'm Tiffany! A former cake designer with a passion for creating products people can enjoy. (Cake as in edible delights. Not to be confused with Cake (C# Make) a build-automation utility.) I am currently a Front End Developer.
Like many people my age, I took HTML and CSS courses in high school. Doing research and a few personal small projects (also using JavaScript) I developed an interest for web development. The thought of turning that into a career seemed so far away at that age. After 11 years in the culinary arts industry, I noticed that there is a surprising amount of overlap of skills in culinary and software development. Some of these transferable skills include, attention to detail, planning/time management, collaboration and problem solving, just to name a few. With this in mind, it rekindled my passion for coding.
I started my journey back to web development by completing NuCamp Coding Bootcamp's "Full Stack" program. After graduating, I continue to challenge myself by taking Udemy courses and creating personal projects. I am actively looking for a web development position where I can collaborate with other developers and put my skills to use.
